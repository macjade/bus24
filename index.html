About us

BUS24 is largest luxurious bus rental company in Nigeria currently managing more than 500 buses in Nigeria. at BUS24, we endeavor to provide Nigerian users the Best in World service and technology at Nigerian prices. BUS24 is currently present in 24 Nigerian cities including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kaduna, Abeokuta, Enugu, Onitsha, Benin City, Aba, Owerri and Warri.

BUS24 gives you a choice of using our amazing buses with your choice of privacy and freedom, so that you can start enjoying your road journey as you wish without waiting to reach your destination. To top it up and be true in our endeavor, BUS24 in Nigeria is available without any kilometer capping, thus offering unlimited kilometers on all bookings made by you. Best things in the World do not come with an usage limit, and unlimited kilometers is our way of telling you that your choice of our buses is among the best in the World. This we believe will be a small step towards making you enjoy your journey to the fullest, by concentrating on counting memories, not kilometers.
